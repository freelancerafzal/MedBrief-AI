MedBrief AI: Clinical Workspace
Your personal, patient-friendly assistant for understanding lab results.

About My Project
MedBrief AI is my very first project, built to solve a personal frustration: why are lab reports so difficult to understand? When you get back blood work or a pathology report, it’s usually full of confusing medical jargon that creates more questions than answers.

I created MedBrief AI to be the bridge between those complex reports and true understanding. It acts like a kind, supportive family doctor, taking dense data and turning it into clear, simple, and reassuring insights.

Why I Built This
Decoding Jargon: I wanted to translate complex medical terminology into plain English so that anyone—regardless of their tech or medical background—can understand their own health data.

Reducing Anxiety: Waiting for doctor follow-ups can be stressful. My goal was to provide immediate, clear, and actionable context so patients feel informed and calm.

Friendly Design: I moved away from "cold" tech dashboards to create a space that feels like a professional, welcoming clinical requisition slip.

What It Can Do
Simple, Three-Step Flow: Just upload your document, process it, and get an explanation. I’ve worked hard to ensure there are no complex tech hurdles.

A Kind AI Persona: I’ve fine-tuned the AI to be patient, warm, and supportive, focusing on "what these numbers mean for you."

Actionable Insights: Instead of just raw data, the tool highlights clear next steps to help you prepare for your actual doctor’s visit.
